Drag the prefab "Little Knight Sir Brom" located in Little Knight Sir Brom/Prefabs/Knight in the scene.

Select it in the hierarchy and then go to the Animator window.

Click on the Paramters tab and you will see two booleans parameters, one called "Left Hand Sword" and one called "Right Hand Sword".

Tick "Left Hand Sword" to place the sword in Sir Braum's left hand, tick "Right Hand Sword" to place the sword in Sir Braum's right hand.

Or tick both to make Sir Braum dual wield!
